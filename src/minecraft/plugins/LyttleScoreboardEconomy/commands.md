## Commands

All of the commands, with explanation.

---

**`/tokens`**

Check your token balance.

*Permission: `tokens.balance`*

---

**`/tokens balance [player]`**

Check another player's token balance.

*Permission: `tokens.balance`*

---

**`/tokens send <player> <amount>`**

Send tokens to other players.

*Permission: `tokens.send`*
